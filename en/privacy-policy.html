Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Lullia ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and protects your information. Lullia is a wellness app designed to help you track your health and mood in relation to barometric pressure changes. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in how your data is handled.

Data We Collect

Lullia collects and processes the following categories of data:

Wellness Data

Health and mood records you create within the app, including daily condition logs, symptom tracking, and personal notes.

Stored via SwiftData + iCloud
Location Data

Your GPS location is used to retrieve local barometric pressure data. You can enable or disable location access in the app's Settings. When disabled, Tokyo's barometric pressure data is used as the default. Location data is cached locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

Local cache only, never sent to servers
Barometric Pressure Data

Atmospheric pressure data is retrieved from the Open-Meteo API based on your location to provide weather-related health insights.

Retrieved from Open-Meteo API
App Settings

Your preferences and configuration settings within the app, such as notification preferences and display options.

Stored locally via UserDefaults
Advertising Data

Lullia uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. The App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework is used to obtain your consent before any tracking for advertising purposes.

Google AdMob with ATT consent
Subscription Information

In-app purchases and subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple through StoreKit. The developer does not have access to your payment information.

Processed by Apple StoreKit
Background Tasks

The app performs background tasks to deliver timely notifications and update barometric pressure data, ensuring you receive relevant health alerts.

For notifications and data updates

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used for the following purposes:

  • Providing core app functionality, including wellness tracking and barometric pressure monitoring
  • Generating analytics and charts to help you visualize health trends over time
  • Sending notifications about significant barometric pressure changes and reminders
  • Displaying relevant advertisements through Google AdMob

Third-Party Services

Lullia integrates with the following third-party services:

Apple iCloud for data synchronization across your devices, and StoreKit for processing in-app purchases and subscriptions.
Open-Meteo Provides barometric pressure and weather data through their public API based on your location.
Google AdMob is used to serve advertisements within the app. Ad personalization respects your ATT preferences.

Data Storage and Security

Your wellness data is stored locally on your device and synchronized through Apple's iCloud service. We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

We do not operate our own servers for storing user data. All personal data remains on your device or within Apple's secure iCloud infrastructure.

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Delete your data at any time directly from within the app
  • Control ad tracking preferences through the App Tracking Transparency (ATT) settings on your device

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When changes are made, you will be notified through the app and updates will be reflected on this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
